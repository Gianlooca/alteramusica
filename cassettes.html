If you aren't Sicilian, actually (probably) from Palermo, you can't get the best from this rare pearl. This cassette is contemporary, just from 1990, but we can consider it already traditional. All real Palermo citizens know by heart at least one of the tracks of this immortal album, which contains comedy skits in a musical form - in the dialect of the town. This is the best. The price speaks for itself.

Romania MC

This is another legend. We are talking about Nicolae Guță magnificul (The magnificent), as they call him sometimes. And this album is one of his first ones so it's pure gold. These cassettes just disappeared from Romania, because they were considered just trash - and treated like trash, directly in the garbage bin. But the musical level, clearly Romani with Romanian nuances, is high. The album has tracks in both Romanian and Romani language, in fact. Do not miss this!

Gianni Celeste 1992
Album VG++/VG+
Neomelodic/Ballad 12€
Italy MC
► More about this

Gianni Celeste is, if not the king, an oligarch of a southern-Italian musical sub-genre called neomelodic. Neomelodic music is strictly sung in neapolitan dialect (even if the singer him/herself isn't from Naples, as in the case of Gianni Celeste who comes from Catania, Sicily) and it's a popular genre among the low classes people. Its songs talk about impossible love, cheating, jail, drug, friendship and also mafia. Considered as trash music by many, yet is listened by many others. Surely an interesting document to know more about the society of southern Italy.
