Kustian 2009
Makalangan VG+/NM
Pop sunda/Folk 10€
Indonesia MC

An interesting contemporary album by an enfant prodige of a productive (and still strong) subgenre of Indonesian music called pop Sunda, that is Sundanese pop. Pop like popular, but still quite exotic for our western ears. We recommend in in particular the main track, "Budak jalanan", that is a street child.

The Balkans are full of singers and bands that have written the history of music in their countries - while abroad are obscure. Thats the case of Mr Mile Kitić and Južni Vetar band. These Serbian guys never miss a beat, and this album is proof of that. If you like late 1980s europop, do not miss this album.

Various Artists 1977
Vol. 10. Calabresi A Milano NM/NM
Italian Urban Folk 25€
Italy LP
► More about this

This is an incredible document of life and dreams of a community of Calabrian immigrants in late 1970s Milan. More than an album, this is field recording of traditional songs played by non-professional artists. This LP is part of a larger musical study about vestigial traditional forms of music in the Lombardia region, published by the perished Italian Albatros label.

Various Artists 1981
Αμανέδες NM/NM
Aman/Rebetiko 10€
Greece LP
► More about this

A reissue with original recordings of rebetiko songs coming out directly from 1920s and 1930s Greek shellacs; in particular, this record contains what were (and still are) called "Amanédes" (pl. of Amanés), kinds of "arrhythmic" improvised sorrow laments of oriental inspiration sung by the best interpreters, such as Antonis Diamantidis "Ntalgas" and Kostas Roukounas.
